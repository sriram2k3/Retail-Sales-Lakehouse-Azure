#Retail-Sales-Lakehouse-Pipeline project
Synthetic Data Design - Business rules

#Customer table
- info data from Jan 1 2026 - July 11 2026
- Customer only in India
- Phone number should not include std code like +91
- customer since date should be within the Jan 2026 - July 2026
- Loyalty tier
	- Gold - 65%
	- Silver - 25%
	- Bronze - 10%

#Product table
- categories within ['Electronics', 'Utensils', 'Food', Clothes' ,'Cosmetics']
- mrp > unit_price > cost_price. price units in INR
- brand and manufacturer can be foreign - no restrictions

# Warehouse table
- warehouses should be within India and inventory near stores
- Cities count can be 2 per State.

#Orders table
- order date can be started after 2 days from Jan 1 2026
- order_status keywords 'Pending', 'Confirmed' ,'Shipped' ,'Out for Delivery', 'Delivered', 'Cancelled', 'Returned'
- planning to show gradually the business is increase over months. So initially Pending and Return should be more than eventually it should be less to showcase
  customer satisfaction indirectly.
- most status delivered, 3% cancelled, 1%returned

#Order items
- qty should be first less from Jan - March then eventually it increases to show profit and customer satisfaction
- line total is INR system
- discount should be based on customer loyalty tier and default product status in inventory to speed up sales and clear old stocks. Max discount should not exceed
  20%
#Payments
- payment_status IN ('Paid','Pending','Failed','Refunded') - 70% Paid, 20% Pending, 7%Refunded, 3%Failed
- payment type IN ('UPI','Card','Cash') - Since this store is getting modernized, initial days payment type is cash then eventually due to flexibility payment
  option other categories also bloomed and platform/stores sees early payments or on spot payment processing speed increased avoid balance amount change issues and
  cash locker maintenance.
